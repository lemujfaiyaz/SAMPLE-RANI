Lyka Estrella

Lyka Estrella was crowned as the sixth grand champion of “Tawag ng Tanghalan '' on “It’s Showtime” after she garnered the highest total combined score from the hurados in the competition’s worldwide trending grandfinals on Saturday (May 6).The resbaker from Gensan received a total combined score of 98.9% inthe final round and beat out her contenders Nowi Alpuerto (95.1%) and Jezza Quiogue (89.6%).
