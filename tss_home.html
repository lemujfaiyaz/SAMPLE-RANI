About General Santos City (Home of Champions)

General Santos City is in the province of South Cotabato in the Philippines. It is a highly urbanized city located at the southern portion of South Cotabato, but perhaps its biggest claim to fame is that boxer Manny Pacquiao is a native.

Aside from Pacquaio they have also produced a lot of talents since then.
